The Social Network Protocol

Internet Bill of Rights

We, the users of social networks, in order to form a more perfect digital union, establish justice, ensure online tranquility, provide for common defense against digital exploitation, promote the general welfare of netizens, and secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our digital posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Social Network Protocol.

Amendment I: The Right to Digital Sovereignty

  • Users have absolute control over their personal data and online identity.
  • No entity, including the platform, shall collect, store, or utilize user data without explicit, informed consent.

Amendment II: Freedom from Algorithmic Manipulation

  • The platform shall not use algorithms that prioritize profit over user well-being or manipulate users' emotions, behaviors, or opinions.
  • Users have the right to opt-out of algorithm-driven content curation and receive transparent, chronological feeds.

Amendment III: Protection from Surveillance Capitalism

  • The platform shall not engage in surveillance capitalism, including but not limited to, targeted advertising based on user data.
  • Users have the right to anonymous browsing and interaction, free from data collection and profiling.

Amendment IV: Right to Transparency and Accountability

  • The platform shall provide transparent and accessible information about its data practices, algorithms, and moderation policies.
  • Users have the right to appeal and redress grievances related to content moderation, data privacy, or algorithmic bias.

Amendment V: Freedom of Expression and Assembly

  • Users have the right to free expression and assembly on the platform, subject only to reasonable limitations necessary to ensure a safe and respectful environment.
  • The platform shall not discriminate against users based on their political beliefs, ideologies, or identities.

Amendment VI: Right to Digital Due Process

  • Users have the right to a fair and transparent process for addressing grievances related to content removal, account suspension, or data breaches.
  • The platform shall provide timely and effective recourse mechanisms for resolving disputes and ensuring accountability.

Amendment VII: Evolution and Adaptation

  • This Constitution shall be reviewed and updated regularly to ensure it remains responsive to emerging technologies, user needs, and societal values.
  • The platform shall engage in open, participatory governance processes, involving users, developers, and civil society organizations, to inform updates and revisions to this Constitution.

By adopting these 7 amendments, the new social network commits to prioritizing user well-being, autonomy, and digital democracy, creating a safe haven for online interaction and fostering a healthier internet ecosystem.
